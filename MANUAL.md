# SSG Lifting Club App - User Manual

Welcome to the official **SSG Lifting Club** Barbell Calculator! This app was built specifically for powerlifters who struggle with quick gym math. Whether you're loading a top set or calculating percentages for your warm-ups, this app has you covered.

## Features at a Glance

### 1. 🏋️‍♂️ Load the Bar (The Core Calculator)
This is the heart of the app. It visualizes the barbell and tells you exactly which plates to load.
- **Calculate Tab:** Enter your target weight (e.g., 315 LB). The app will instantly calculate the plates you need to load on *each side* of the bar and draw them on the screen.
- **Reverse Tab:** Not sure how much is on the bar? Switch to the Reverse tab. Simply tap the plates that you see on the physical barbell, and the app will calculate the total weight for you.
- **Warm Up Tab:** Enter your top set for the day in the Calculate tab, then switch to Warm Up. The app will automatically generate an intelligent 5-step warm-up protocol (Empty Bar, 50%, 70%, 80%, 90%) mathematically rounded to the exact plates you have available! Click the "Load" button next to any warm-up set to instantly visualize it.

### 2. 📋 Inventory Management
Not every gym has competition plates. In the **Inventory** tab, you can customize exactly which plates you have access to. 
- You can turn plates on or off (e.g., if your gym doesn't have 55 lb plates, just disable them).
- The calculator will automatically adjust its math based *only* on the plates you have enabled.

### 3. 🏆 Personal Records (PR) Tracker
Track your all-time heaviest lifts directly on your device.
- Add your Squat, Bench Press, Deadlift, or any custom lift.
- Your records are saved directly to your phone's `localStorage`, meaning they are **100% private** and work entirely offline without needing an account.
- **Quick Action:** Click the "Load" button next to any PR to instantly send that weight to the calculator so you can load it up!

### 4. 🧮 Powerlifting Calculators
- **1RM Calculator (Brzycki):** Enter the weight and the number of reps you performed. The app will estimate your 1 Rep Max.
- **RPE Calculator:** Use the RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) calculator to determine how much weight you should put on the bar based on your target RPE and reps.

### 5. ⚙️ Settings & Customization
- **Weight Units:** Seamlessly toggle between LB and KG. You can also use **Mixed Mode** to calculate KG plates for a target LB weight (or vice versa).
- **Rounding:** Change how the math rounds (1, 1.1, 1.25).
- **Themes:** Make the app your own! Choose between SSG Red, Pitch Black, Space Grey, and Clean White themes. The entire app's aesthetic will adapt instantly.
